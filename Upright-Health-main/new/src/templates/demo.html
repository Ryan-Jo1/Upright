Personalized Posture Analysis for Patient Care

This posture-tracking app lets you input your condition, then activates the webcam with a 15-second prep time. It monitors your posture for 25 minutes, providing real-time alerts for misalignment. Afterward, it analyzes your posture and offers personalized feedback. You then perform corrective exercises, with the system ensuring proper form—especially tracking waist alignment—to help improve your posture over time.
